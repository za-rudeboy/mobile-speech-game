# Simple Mobile Game Spec: “First, Then, Because”

## Goal

A simple visual language game to help a child practise:

- **because**
- **first**
- **then**
- **before**
- **after**

The game shows short picture stories and asks the child to choose, arrange, or explain what happened.

---

## Game Format

Each level uses **2 or 3 images**.

The child sees a question, looks at the pictures, and either:

1. taps the correct image,
2. drags images into order,
3. chooses a sentence,
4. or repeats after Caelum.

Caelum should model the answer verbally and visually.

---

## Core Interaction Pattern

### Example 1: Because

#### Question

**Why is the boy crying?**

#### Images

**Image A:** Boy happily holding an ice cream.  
**Image B:** Boy crying, ice cream dropped on the floor.

#### Caelum answer

> He is crying **because** he dropped his ice cream.

#### Learning target

Cause and effect using **because**.

#### Optional prompt

> What happened?  
> The ice cream fell.  
> Why is he sad?  
> Because the ice cream fell.

---

### Example 2: First / Then

#### Concept

**Wash hands and then eat.**

#### Question

**What do we do first?**

#### Images to use

**Image A:** Child washing hands at a sink with soap and water.  
**Image B:** Child sitting at a table eating food.

#### Caelum answer

> **First** we wash hands. **Then** we eat.

#### Learning target

Daily routine sequencing.

#### Optional follow-up

> What happens **after** washing hands?  
> We eat.

> What happens **before** eating?  
> We wash hands.

---

### Example 3: Before / After

#### Concept

**Put on socks before shoes.**

#### Question

**What comes before shoes?**

#### Images

**Image A:** Child putting on socks.  
**Image B:** Child putting on shoes.

#### Caelum answer

> Socks come **before** shoes.  
> **First** socks, **then** shoes.

#### Learning target

Understanding **before** and **then** using a familiar routine.

---

## Suggested Level Structure

### Level 1: First / Then

Use two images.

Example:

> **First** brush teeth. **Then** go to bed.

The child drags the pictures into the correct order.

---

### Level 2: After

Use two images.

Example:

> What happens **after** washing hands?

Correct image:

> Eating food.

Caelum says:

> We eat **after** washing hands.

---

### Level 3: Before

Use two images.

Example:

> What happens **before** eating?

Correct image:

> Washing hands.

Caelum says:

> We wash hands **before** eating.

---

### Level 4: Because

Use two images showing a cause and result.

Example:

> Why is the floor wet?

Images:

- cup falling over
- wet floor

Caelum says:

> The floor is wet **because** the cup spilled.

---

### Level 5: Mixed Practice

Use three images.

Example:

1. Boy runs.
2. Boy falls.
3. Boy cries.

Caelum asks:

> What happened first?  
> What happened then?  
> Why is the boy crying?

Expected answer:

> **First** he ran. **Then** he fell. He is crying **because** he fell.

---

## Game Screen Layout

### Top

Question text:

> Why is the boy crying?

### Middle

Two or three large picture cards.

### Bottom

Answer buttons or draggable sentence pieces:

- because
- first
- then
- before
- after

For early levels, Caelum can provide sentence frames:

> He is crying because ______.

or

> First ______, then ______.

---

## Feedback Style

Keep feedback gentle and repetitive.

### Correct answer

> Yes! He is crying **because** he dropped his ice cream.

### Incorrect answer

> Good try. Look again. What happened to the ice cream?

Then highlight the correct image.

---

## Good Picture Sets to Create

1. **Ice cream drops → boy cries**  
   Target: because

2. **Wash hands → eat food**  
   Target: first, then, before, after

3. **Brush teeth → go to bed**  
   Target: first, then

4. **Rain falls → girl uses umbrella**  
   Target: because

5. **Cup spills → table is wet**  
   Target: because

6. **Put on socks → put on shoes**  
   Target: before, after

7. **Plant seed → water seed → flower grows**  
   Target: first, then, after

8. **Child runs → child falls → child cries**  
   Target: first, then, because

---

## MVP Scope

For the first version, keep it very simple:

- 10 picture stories
- 2-image and 3-image cards
- tap-to-answer
- drag-to-order for sequencing
- spoken Caelum model answer
- no scoring pressure
- lots of repetition

---

## Key Sentence Frames

> **First** _____. **Then** _____.

> _____ happened **after** _____.

> _____ happened **before** _____.

> He/she is _____ **because** _____.

---

## Notes on the “Wash Hands Then Eat” Illustration

For the second example, the best two images are:

1. **A child washing hands at a sink**
2. **The same child eating at a table**

This pair supports four useful questions:

> What happens first?  
> What happens then?  
> What happens before eating?  
> What happens after washing hands?
